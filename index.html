Professional Summary

I am a PhD pharmaceutical researcher with a strong academic foundation, holding a Bachelor of Pharmacy followed by an MSc in Pharmaceutics and advanced drug-delivery systems. My research focuses on translating pharmaceutical science into practical, innovative solutions.

I have authored and co-authored more than a dozen peer-reviewed research publications in advanced drug-delivery systems, 3D bioprinted tumor models, and cancer chemosensitization using novel therapeutic compounds, contributing to precision medicine.

In parallel, I am a programmer and full-stack web developer, building research-driven digital platforms that bridge pharmaceutical science with modern web technology.
